Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

We build kids-friendly learning applications. This privacy policy describes how your information is handled when you download and use the **ABC Alphabet Trace & Learn** mobile app on Android devices.

1. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our app is specifically designed for toddlers, preschoolers, and young children. We strictly comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not collect, request, or store any personally identifiable information (PII) from children.

2. Data Collection & Usage

  • Personal Data: We do not collect any personal info (such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or GPS location).
  • Voice Recordings (Parent Voice feature): The custom voice recordings you record in the app are stored 100% locally on your own physical device. We do not transmit, upload, or share your recordings with any servers.
  • Progress Storage: Your child's high scores, tracing progress, and unlocked brush stars are stored locally on the device using local storage (Hive databases).

3. Third-Party Services & Advertising

To keep the app free, we display age-appropriate ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process device advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID) to serve non-personalized, family-safe ads. All ad requests sent from this app are flagged as child-directed (using Google's TFCD and Tag For Child Directed Treatment options) to ensure maximum privacy protection for kids.

4. Data Security

Since your child's learning progress and audio recordings are stored entirely on your device, your data security is protected by your device's security system. No online servers are used, minimizing data breach risks.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email: farook.dev@example.com
